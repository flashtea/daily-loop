BlackRock's BITA yield Bitcoin ETF begins trading on Nasdaq today

BlackRock's iShares Bitcoin Premium Income ETF (ticker: BITA) launched on Nasdaq this morning — the first covered-call yield ETF on Bitcoin. BITA targets 15–25% annual yield by selling covered calls on IBIT, charges a 0.65% sponsor fee, and holds bitcoin exposure through direct BTC custody at Coinbase combined with IBIT shares. BlackRock filed the Form 8-A on June 11, beating Goldman Sachs to market by roughly a month.

Why it matters — Yield-seeking institutions can now access BTC income without holding spot; expands the institutional investor base before Goldman's rival product arrives in July.

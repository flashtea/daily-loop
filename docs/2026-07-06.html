UN opens its first Global Dialogue on AI Governance in Geneva

All 193 UN member states, alongside industry and civil-society representatives, convened in Geneva for a two-day inaugural session on international AI governance, with Secretary-General António Guterres warning that AI is "already" transforming the world.

Why it matters — The first standing UN mechanism meant to give governments a shared forum for AI rules, rather than each bloc regulating in isolation.

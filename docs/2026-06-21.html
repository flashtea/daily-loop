US export directive keeps Claude Fable 5 offline; Anthropic vows restoration 'in days'

Cloudflare acquires VoidZero, bringing Vite, Vitest, and Rolldown in-house

Franklin Templeton files for Bitcoin DRIP ETFs that convert stock dividends into BTC

Franklin Templeton filed with the SEC on June 19 for two novel ETFs—the Franklin US Equity Bitcoin DRIP Index ETF and the Franklin US Innovation Bitcoin DRIP Index ETF—that hold large-cap US equities while automatically reinvesting all dividend income into Bitcoin instead of shares. Both funds target a September 1 launch date pending SEC review.

Why it matters — A novel structure that would systematically route equity dividend flows into BTC, expanding institutional exposure without a direct purchase mandate on investors.
